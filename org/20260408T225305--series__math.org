* Series

A series can be represented in the form:
\[\sum^\infty_{n=1}a_n\]

** Types of Series

*** Harmonic Series

\[\sum^\infty_{n=1}\frac{1}{n}\]

**NOTE**: This series is always divergent!

*** Telescoping Series

\(\sum^\infty_{n=1}(\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n+1})\)

*** Geometric Series

\[
\sum^\infty_{n=1}ar^{n-1}
\]

Value of convergence can be found with the formula:
\(\frac{a}{1-r}\)

- Converges if : \(|r| < 1\)
- Diverges if : \(|r| \geq 1\)

*** P-Series

\[\sum^\infty_{n=1}\frac{1}{n^p}
\]

- Converges if : \(p < 1\)
- Diverges if : \(p \geq 1\)
