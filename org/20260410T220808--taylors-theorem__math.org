* Taylor’s Theorem

Suppose f(x) is differentiable \(n+1\) times out of \(x_0\).
If \[|f^{(n+1)}(x)|\leq M\] on an interval containing \(x_0\),
say \(a \leq x_0 \leq b\), then the remainder \(R_n(x)\) satisfies the following
inequality:
\[|R_N(x)| \leq \frac{M}{(n+1)!}|x-x_0|^{n+1}\]
for all \(x\)-values  in \([a,b]\).

Note that \(M\) is the maximum on the interval.
