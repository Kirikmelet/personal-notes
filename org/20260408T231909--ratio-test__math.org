* Ratio Test

** Theorem

Let \(\sum^\infty_{k=1}a_k\) be a series with non-zero terms and \(\rho=\lim_{k\rightarrow\infty}\frac{a_{k+1}}{a_k}\).

1. If \(\rho < 1\), the series \(\sum^\infty_{k=1}a_k\) converges absolutely and therefore converges.
2. If \(\rho>1\) or \(\rho=+\infty\), the series \(\sum^\infty_{k=1}a_k\) diverges.
3. If \(\rho=1\), the test is inconclusive and no conclusion can be drawn.
