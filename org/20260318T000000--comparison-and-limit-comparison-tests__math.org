* Comparison and Limit  Comparison Tests

**  Theorem (Comparison Test)

Suppose \(\sum^\infty_{n=1} a_n\) and \(\sum^{\infty}_{n=1} b_n\) are series with non-negative terms.

1. If \(\sum^{\infty}_{n=1} b_n\) is convergent and \(a_n \leq b_n\) for all \(n\), then \(\sum^{\infty}_{n=1} a_n\) is convergent.
2. If \(\sum^{\infty}_{n=1} a_n\) is divergent and \(a_n \leq b_n\) for all \(n\), then \(\sum^{\infty}_{n=1} b_n\) is divergent.
