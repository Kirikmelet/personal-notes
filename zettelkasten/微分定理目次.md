# 微分定理目次


## ロルの定理 

1. $f(x)$ is continuous within $[a,b]$
2. $f(x)$ is differentiable within $(a,b)$
3. $f(a)=f(b)$

## 平均値の定理

Follow up on [ロルの定理](#ロルの定理).
Difference is that $f(a)=f(b)$ does not have to be true.

1. $f(x)$ is continuous within $[a,b]$
2. $f(x)$ is differentiable within $(a,b)$
3. $f'(c)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$ for value $c$ in $[a,b]$
