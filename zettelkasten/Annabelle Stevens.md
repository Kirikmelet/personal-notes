# Annabelle Stevens

> [!info] 
> - Sex: Female
> - Age: 22 (1941-)
> - Nationality: Bloxian
> - Education: Emerald Eard University, Mallowfield
> - Occupation: Squad Leader, Emerald Eardfird

Annabelle Stevens (1941-), also known as Anna, was a Squad Leader of the Commonwealth Armed Forces. Annabelle was raised in the rural city of Mallowfield, Emerald Eard,
but was born in the City of Springfield, Bloxian Noobia to a Noobic mother and Bloxian father.

She was present during the West-East Noobic Conflict of 1961 while in the CAF 171st Infantry Regiment.


## Early Life

Annabelle was born in the city of Springfield, Springfield Province, Bloxian Colony of Noobia at the General Municipal Hospital on 2nd Februrary, 1941.
She was born to a Richard Stevens, a Bloxian engineer, and to Karen Woods, a Noobic teacher.
Annabelle had a twin sister, [Elizabeth Stevens](Elizabeth%20Stevens.md).

Annabelle initally entered the 2nd Springfield Municipal Kindergarten, where she attended for 3 months before the outbreak of the Noobic Independence War.
The outbreak of the war caused the family to split; Annabelle and her father repatriated to Bloxia, and her twin-sister and mother relocated to Naramo City.
Annabelle and her father settled at the father's hometown, Mallowfield City in the Emerald Eard.
She resumed her education at the Spring Brooks Primary School.

## Military Career

Furthermore, Annabelle saw deployment in the raid of the Naramo Nuclear Power Plant during the Noobic Reunification War (1962-1967) as part of an expeditionary unit of the 171st Infantry Regiment alongside the 21st Infantry Battalion of the West Noobic Federal Ground Defense Force against the local 1st Noobic Division garrison alongside the 14th Armored Cavalry Regiment and infantry elements of the 3rd Noobic Division and naval infantry of the 1st Noobic Fleet.
