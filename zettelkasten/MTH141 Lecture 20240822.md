# MTH141 Lecture 20240822

## Quadratic Formula 二次方程式の解の公式

For $ax^2+bx+c=0$, the solution(s) is:
$$x=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$$
This is known as the **Quadratic Formula**.
