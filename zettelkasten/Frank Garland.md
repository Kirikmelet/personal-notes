# Frank Garland

Frank Garland (1935-), was a Senior Patrol Officer of the Noobic Military Police. Frank was born in the Naramo District to a middle-class family in the city of Naramo, Naramo District. Frank shortly graduated from the Noobic Military Police Academy before receiving permanent orders to patrol the strategic Naramo Nuclear Power Plant from saboteurs and traitors during the Noobic Reunification War (1962-1967). 

Frank Garland was deployed with the other Noobic Military Police officers stationed in Naramo alongside elements of the 13th Armored Cavalry Regiment, local garrison of the 1st Noobic Division, reservists of the 3rd Noobic Division, and naval infantry of the 1st Noobic Fleet and detachment of Noobic Marine Corps against the 21 Infantry Regiment of the Noobic Self Defense Force alongside their advisors from the Bloxian 171st Infantry Regiment during the Raid of the Naramo Nuclear Power Plant (1963).
