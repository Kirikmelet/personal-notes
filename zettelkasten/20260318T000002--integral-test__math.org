* Integral

** Theorem

Suppose \(f\) is a continuous, positive, decreasing function on \([1,\infty)\) and let \(a_n=f(n)\) then,

1. If \(\int^\infty_1 f(x)dx\) is convergent, then \(\sum^\infty_{n=1} a_n\) converges.
2. If \(\int^\infty_1 f(x)dx\) is divergent, then \(\sum^\infty_{n=1} a_n\) diverges.
